User Research B3 — DPW Communications Staff

Build a detailed user persona for DPW communications staff members who answer resident questions about public works projects and service schedules. Research:

- What types of resident inquiries do DPW communications staff most commonly receive (project status, parking, service schedules, complaints)?
- What tools and resources do communications staff currently use to answer these inquiries?
- What information do they frequently lack or have to chase down from operations colleagues?
- How much time is spent per day or week on repetitive project-status or service-schedule inquiries?
- What would a communications staff member need from a self-service resident tool vs. an internal lookup tool?
- How do communications staff currently update the public during storm events or major service disruptions?

Output:
- Persona sketch with workflow, common queries, and tool requirements
- Facts (with source titles)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Design implications for communications-oriented tools

Prior Art E3 — How 311 Systems Handle Public Works Requests

Research how 311 systems in mid-size American cities handle public works service requests related to construction, road conditions, and service schedules.

Investigate:
- What categories of public works requests are most common in 311 systems (potholes, construction complaints, service schedule questions, road closures)?
- How do cities use 311 data to improve public works communication and transparency?
- Are there examples of cities that have connected 311 data to infrastructure project maps?
- How does Richmond's 311 system (if it exists) handle public works requests?
- What can a hackathon team learn from 311 data patterns without accessing raw 311 call data?

Apply to Richmond:
- Does Richmond publish 311 request data on the Open Data Portal?
- Can 311 category data reveal which blocks or neighborhoods have the highest demand for project information?

Output:
- Facts (with source titles)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Potential use of 311 data in an infrastructure visibility tool
- Limitations and privacy considerations

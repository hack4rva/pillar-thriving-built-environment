User Research B2 — The DPW Operations Supervisor

Build a detailed user persona for a DPW operations supervisor managing snow removal or street cleaning routes. Research:

- What does a DPW supervisor's workday look like during a storm event or street cleaning cycle?
- What information do supervisors need most urgently and how do they currently get it?
- What are the pain points of manual check-in and radio-based route tracking?
- How do supervisors currently communicate completion status to City leadership and communications staff?
- What would a supervisor actually use vs. what would they ignore from a digital tool?
- What are the technical literacy constraints for a tool designed for operations supervisors?
- What resistance or adoption barriers might exist for a new tracking tool?

Output:
- Persona sketch with workflow, pain points, and tool requirements
- Facts (with source titles)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Design implications for a supervisor-facing dashboard

Services Landscape C1 — DPW Structure, Programs, and Public Communication

Research the structure of Richmond's Department of Public Works and its current public communication channels. Using verifiable public information:

- What divisions or programs exist within Richmond DPW relevant to transportation and infrastructure?
- What are the main active transportation programs (streetscape, safety improvements, mobility, etc.)?
- How does DPW currently communicate active projects to the public (website, social media, 311, press releases)?
- What is the relationship between DPW and the City's communications/public affairs function?
- What is the relationship between DPW and VDOT for roads within Richmond city limits?
- What contact channels exist for residents to ask questions or report issues?

Apply specifically to Richmond, Virginia. Do not generalize from other cities.

Output:
- Facts (with source titles)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
- Gaps between current communication channels and resident needs

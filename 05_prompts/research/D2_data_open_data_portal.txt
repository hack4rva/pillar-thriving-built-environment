Data Inventory D2 — Richmond Open Data Portal DPW Datasets

Inventory the datasets on Richmond's Open Data Portal (data.richmondgov.com) that are relevant to DPW and transportation.

For each dataset category, research:
- Whether such datasets exist on the portal (verified or inferred)
- Dataset name or likely dataset name
- Key fields and data types
- Update frequency
- Access method (Socrata API, CSV download)
- Data quality considerations

Dataset categories to investigate:
- Capital improvement projects
- 311 service requests (filter for construction/road/public works categories)
- Road maintenance and resurfacing records
- Street cleaning schedules or completion records
- Permit activity for construction
- Traffic incident data adjacent to construction zones

Output:
- Verified datasets (with source titles)
- Inferences about dataset existence (labeled)
- Unknowns to verify on the portal before building
- Recommended datasets for the hackathon with rationale

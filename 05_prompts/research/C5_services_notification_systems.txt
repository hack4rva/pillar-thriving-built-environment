Services Landscape C5 — How Richmond Currently Notifies Residents About Public Works

Research how Richmond currently uses notification systems for public works communications.

- Does Richmond have an opt-in notification system for residents (GovDelivery, Notify RVA, Nextdoor, etc.)?
- How are residents currently notified before a construction project begins near them?
- How are residents currently notified about street cleaning schedules?
- How are storm communications (snow removal updates) currently published?
- What notification channels are used (email, SMS, social media, website banners, press releases)?
- What gaps exist between these notification channels and resident awareness?
- What notification infrastructure already exists that a hackathon prototype could build on or complement?

Output:
- Facts (with source titles)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Existing notification assets a hackathon tool could connect to
- Gaps that a prototype notification system could address

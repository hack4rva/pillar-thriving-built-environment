User Research B4 — Stakeholder Map

Map all stakeholders who have an interest in infrastructure project transparency and fleet operations visibility in Richmond. For each stakeholder:

- Primary interest (what do they want to know or do?)
- Current access to information (high/medium/low)
- Potential benefit from a transparency tool
- Potential concern or risk from a transparency tool

Stakeholder categories to cover:
- Richmond residents (various neighborhoods and demographics)
- DPW operations staff (supervisors, drivers, planners)
- DPW communications and public affairs
- City Council members and constituent staff
- Neighborhood associations and civic groups
- Nonprofit partners (Groundwork RVA, James River Association, Partnership for Smarter Growth)
- Local media and journalists
- Business owners (affected by construction, parking, road closures)
- Accessibility advocates (residents with mobility limitations)
- GRTC transit riders (bus service affected by road conditions and construction)

Output:
- Stakeholder table with columns: stakeholder, primary interest, current access, potential benefit, potential concern
- Key tensions or tradeoffs between stakeholder interests

Prior Art E2 — Street Service Status Tools (Snow Removal and Street Cleaning)

Research the best examples of public-facing city tools for communicating snow removal and street cleaning status to residents.

For each example, describe:
- City and tool name
- What it does (live tracking vs. zone status vs. schedule display)
- Data source (GPS required or not? zone data?)
- Key UX features
- Known strengths and limitations
- Whether the tool works without real-time GPS

Tools to investigate specifically:
- Chicago Plow Tracker
- NYC Sanitation street cleaning map
- Boston Winter Ready / SnowWatch
- Montgomery County MD SnowWatch
- Denver snow plow tracker
- Pittsburgh snow removal status
- Any Virginia examples

Emphasize: which of these tools can be replicated WITHOUT live GPS data? Which require GPS? What is the minimum viable version of each?

Output:
- Comparison table: city, tool, GPS required, zone-based?, schedule-based?, resident value
- Recommendation for which model is achievable in 48 hours without live GPS
- Key design patterns for zone-based status display

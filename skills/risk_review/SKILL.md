# risk_review

Purpose: Prevent common civic-tech mistakes and enforce guardrails.

## When To Use
- Before sharing claims publicly, shipping copy, or demoing.
- When GPS, eligibility, legal, or service claims appear.

## Inputs
- Product copy, UI text, README/marketing claims, demo script.
- Research outputs and cited sources.

## Outputs
- List of flagged risks: hallucinated services, GPS overclaims, eligibility/legal claims, missing/weak sources, misleading language.
- Concrete fixes: rewordings, source additions, scope trims.
- Any open questions to resolve with officials.

## Process
1) Check all civic claims for official sources; require links.
2) Flag any claim that GPS data is live, available, or real — replace with explicit mock/synthetic labeling.
3) Remove/soften eligibility and legal advice; replace with "check official source".
4) Verify Richmond specificity; avoid generalizing across jurisdictions.
5) Ensure weekend‑feasible scope; cut features that require new policy or inaccessible data.
6) Summarize fixes and request confirmation to apply changes.

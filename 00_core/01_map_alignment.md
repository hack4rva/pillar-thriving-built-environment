# MAP Alignment — A Thriving and Sustainable Built Environment

## Pillar position in the Richmond MAP framework
Pillar 6: A Thriving and Sustainable Built Environment is one of seven pillars in Richmond's MAP (Master Action Plan) framework. It focuses on the physical infrastructure and environment that shapes how residents experience the city.

## How this pillar connects to other MAP pillars
- **Pillar 1 (Connected and Engaged Communities):** Infrastructure project transparency directly supports civic engagement — residents who can find and understand project information are more able to participate in planning processes.
- **Pillar 4 (Thriving Economy):** Transportation infrastructure improvements shape where businesses locate, who can reach jobs, and the operational costs of commercial activity.
- **Pillar 7 (Resilient City):** Sustainable design standards and climate-resilient infrastructure investments are closely linked to this pillar's sustainability theme.
